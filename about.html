Biography

My name is Misty and I am originally from Danville, IL but I have lived in Roswell, NM for 10 years now. I served in the United States Army for a little over 4 years and that is how I met my wonderful husband. Together we have two dogs Lexi and Sadie or I as like to call them fur-babies. In my free time you can find me gardening, exercising outdoors, jet skiing at the lake, reading, painting, or cooking. I am also an amateur photographer because I love to be able to capture moments in life and look back on them. I also love to travel and capture all of the places that I visit. it is something I enjoy and it helps to keep me involved in technology.
